Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common dental questions from our patients.

1. How often should I visit a dentist?

We recommend visiting every 6 months for routine checkups and professional cleanings. This helps prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral health issues.

2. How much does a dental check-up cost in Addis Ababa?

Our check-ups are affordable and transparent. Contact us for current pricing—no hidden charges.

3. Do you offer teeth whitening services?

Yes. Our safe, professional whitening treatments remove stains and brighten your smile, often in just one visit.

4. Are braces or Invisalign available at your clinic?

We offer both traditional braces and Invisalign clear aligners, with personalized treatment plans for your needs.

5. What should I do in a dental emergency?

If you experience severe pain, swelling, or a broken tooth, call us immediately for emergency dental care.

6. Do you accept insurance or Telebirr?

Yes, we accept select insurance providers and Telebirr payments. Contact us to confirm your coverage.
